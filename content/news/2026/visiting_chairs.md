Hi! PARIS has announced its 2026 International Visiting Chairs program, welcoming eleven international professors to collaborate on artificial intelligence and data science projects with member schools in France. Among the selected researchers is Gustau Camps-Valls from the Universitat de València, who will work on the project "AI4ES: Causal & Equation Discovery for Earth Science," hosted by Rémi Flamary at École polytechnique (IP Paris).
